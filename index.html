1. Go to Exercises, and record reps of your exercises (It may take some time for program to recognize body parts).

2. Go to Routines, click on Edit Routines, and then Add Routine.

3. Add exercises with sets and reps, name your routine and add it.

4. Go back to Routines, and select your routine in days of the week.

5. Start your routine (Be sure to place camera at the same angle where you recorded your exercise from).
